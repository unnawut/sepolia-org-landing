Sepolia.org is discontinued

After [3 years of service](https://x.com/TimBeiko/status/1536836142644940800) and all its costs, I've decided to discontinue sepolia.org along with all its RPCs. You can find [many other sepolia RPCs now at chainlist.org](https://chainlist.org/chain/11155111)

An independently-run toolset for Sepolia testnet. It all started from Tim Beiko's bounty for a reliable Sepolia RPC.

  • RPC (Asia): https://rpc.sepolia.org
  • RPC2 (Europe): https://rpc2.sepolia.org
  • Status Page: https://status.sepolia.org
